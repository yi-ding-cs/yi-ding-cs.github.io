Biography

I received my Ph.D. from the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE) at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2023, supervised by Prof Guan Cuntai. Before that, I received my MSc from the School of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2018, supervised by Prof Chen Tupei. Previously, I earned my B.Eng degree from the School of Information Science and Technology at Donghua University, Shanghai, China in 2017.

My research interests include brain-computer interface, deep/machine learning, graph neural networks, affective computing, computational neuroscience, multimodal emotion recognition, and their applications in neural signal decoding and mental disorder regulation.
